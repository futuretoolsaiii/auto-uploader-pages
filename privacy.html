Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 15, 2025

AutoUploader ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • TikTok account ID and profile information (via TikTok OAuth)
  • Uploaded video metadata (title, hashtags)
  • Access tokens (stored temporarily and securely)

We do not collect passwords or private content from your TikTok account.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Authenticate users via TikTok
  • Upload and publish videos on your behalf
  • Improve our services and user experience

3. Data Retention

We retain access tokens only for the duration of the user session. No content or tokens are permanently stored on our servers.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or share your information with third parties. Your data is only used to interact with TikTok's API as required to provide the service.

5. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You may revoke AutoUploader’s access to your TikTok account at any time via TikTok’s settings. You may also contact us to request data deletion.

7. Third-Party Services

Our service relies on TikTok APIs. Please refer to TikTok's Privacy Policy for more information on how TikTok handles your data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by updating the effective date at the top of this page.
